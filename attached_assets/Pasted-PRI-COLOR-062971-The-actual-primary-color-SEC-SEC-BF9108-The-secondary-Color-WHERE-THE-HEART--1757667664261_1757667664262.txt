38 Bible verses about mercy and compassion

An amazing selection of verses about God’s nature, the incredible consequences this has for us, and the responsibility we are given.

and fun facts:
>>>Exodus 34:6-7 – “The Lord, the Lord God, merciful and gracious…”
🟡 Fun fact: This is the most-quoted verse about God’s character — even other Bible writers repeat it (e.g., Psalm 103:8, Joel 2:13). It’s God’s own “bio.”

Deuteronomy 7:9 – God keeps mercy for a thousand generations.
🟡 Fun fact: A thousand generations would cover over 30,000 years — meaning His mercy is effectively limitless.

2 Samuel 22:26 – “With the merciful You will show Yourself merciful.”
🟡 Fun fact: This verse is also in Psalm 18:25 — David liked it so much, he sang it twice!

Psalm 25:10 – “All the paths of the Lord are mercy and truth.”
🟡 Fun fact: Mercy + truth is one of the Bible’s favorite “duos” — they appear together over 30 times.

Psalm 86:15 – God is “full of compassion.”
🟡 Fun fact: David says this in the middle of a prayer for help — compassion isn’t just a feeling; it’s what gives us hope.

Psalm 89:14 – “Mercy and truth go before Your face.”
🟡 Fun fact: This means mercy leads God’s every action — before judgment, before justice.

Psalm 145:8-9 – “The Lord is gracious and full of compassion.”
🟡 Fun fact: This is the only psalm where every verse starts with a new letter of the Hebrew alphabet — an ABC of praise.

Lamentations 3:22-23 – “His compassions are new every morning.”
🟡 Fun fact: Jeremiah wrote this while watching Jerusalem in ruins — proving compassion shines brightest in despair.

Micah 7:18-19 – God casts our sins into the sea.
🟡 Fun fact: In Jewish tradition, people toss bread into water during Yom Kippur to symbolize this verse — literally “throwing away” sins.

John 3:16 – God gave His Son out of love.
🟡 Fun fact: This is the most translated Bible verse in history — over 1,600 languages and counting.

Ephesians 2:4-5 – “God, who is rich in mercy…”
🟡 Fun fact: Paul says God isn’t just merciful — He’s “rich” in it. Mercy is God’s wealth!

Mark 6:34 – Jesus was moved with compassion and taught them.
🟡 Fun fact: Jesus’ compassion often led Him to teach, not just heal — because truth is the deepest form of help.

Hebrews 2:17-18 – Jesus is a merciful High Priest.
🟡 Fun fact: High Priests offered sacrifices for sin — Jesus offered Himself, the ultimate compassionate act.

Hebrews 4:14-16 – We can “come boldly to the throne of grace.”
🟡 Fun fact: Ancient kings’ thrones meant fear — God’s throne invites confidence and mercy-seeking.

📖 Verses about Our Call to Show Compassion

Luke 6:36 – “Be merciful, just as your Father also is merciful.”
🟡 Fun fact: This is like Jesus saying, “Act like your Dad!” Mercy runs in the family.

2 Corinthians 1:3-4 – God comforts us so we can comfort others.
🟡 Fun fact: This is the ultimate “pay-it-forward” verse — comfort is meant to circulate.

Job 6:14 – “To him who is afflicted, kindness should be shown…”
🟡 Fun fact: This is Job gently telling his friends, “Guys, maybe try sympathy instead of lectures?”

Psalm 112:4 – “Light arises in the darkness.”
🟡 Fun fact: This verse calls compassion a light — a kind heart literally brightens dark situations.

Proverbs 3:3-4 – Bind mercy around your neck.
🟡 Fun fact: Think of this as wearing compassion like a necklace — a permanent accessory.

Proverbs 11:17 – “The merciful man does good for his own soul.”
🟡 Fun fact: Helping others is scientifically proven to release endorphins — a Biblical “feel-good” hack.

Proverbs 12:10 – “A righteous man regards the life of his animal.”
🟡 Fun fact: This is one of the earliest pro-animal-care verses in history — centuries before modern animal rights.

Proverbs 14:21 – “He who has mercy on the poor, happy is he.”
🟡 Fun fact: Compassion is linked to happiness — charity is good for mental health!

Proverbs 14:31 – Honoring God = mercy on the needy.
🟡 Fun fact: This verse basically says, “How you treat the poor is how you treat God.”

Micah 6:8 – “Love mercy, walk humbly.”
🟡 Fun fact: This is one of the simplest “mission statements” of the whole Bible.

Zechariah 7:8-10 – Show mercy and compassion to everyone.
🟡 Fun fact: This was said after the Israelites had returned from exile — compassion was key to rebuilding community.

Matthew 5:7 – “Blessed are the merciful…”
🟡 Fun fact: Mercy is one of the only Beatitudes with a built-in reward — you get what you give.

Matthew 7:1-2 – Judge not, so you won’t be judged.
🟡 Fun fact: The most misquoted verse in pop culture — but it’s really about showing mercy.

Matthew 7:12 – The Golden Rule.
🟡 Fun fact: Almost every major religion has a version of this — mercy is a universal language.

Matthew 23:23 – Jesus calls mercy a “weightier matter of the law.”
🟡 Fun fact: He ranked mercy higher than rituals — love > legalism.

Matthew 25:35-40, 45 – “I was hungry and you gave Me food…”
🟡 Fun fact: This is Jesus identifying with the needy so closely that helping them is helping Him.

Galatians 6:2 – “Bear one another’s burdens.”
🟡 Fun fact: This is like spiritual weightlifting — we’re all personal trainers for each other’s souls.

Galatians 6:7-10 – “Whatever a man sows, that he will also reap.”
🟡 Fun fact: Compassion is seed-planting — expect a harvest of kindness back.

Ephesians 4:31-32 – “Be kind to one another…”
🟡 Fun fact: The Greek word for “tenderhearted” literally means “having strong bowels” — compassion was felt in the gut!

Colossians 3:12-14 – Put on tender mercies like clothes.
🟡 Fun fact: This is a daily “dress code” — you can actually choose to wear compassion every morning.

1 Thessalonians 5:14-15 – Be patient with all.
🟡 Fun fact: The command covers everyone — even that annoying person in class.

James 2:13 – “Mercy triumphs over judgment.”
🟡 Fun fact: Mercy is pictured as winning a court case — the final verdict is grace.

James 3:17 – Wisdom is “full of mercy.”
🟡 Fun fact: True wisdom isn’t just smart — it’s kind.

1 Peter 3:8-9 – Have compassion for one another.
🟡 Fun fact: This was written to persecuted believers — compassion was how they stayed united under pressure.
